Description

Dive into an epic adventure on the high seas with Raft Wars 1! Join Simon and his brother as they defend their treasure against greedy pirates and other foes. Armed with a trusty slingshot, you must aim and shoot projectiles to defeat enemies and reclaim what is rightfully yours. Navigate through challenging levels filled with obstacles and clever adversaries as you strive to emerge victorious in this exciting water-based battle!

Controls:

Use the mouse to aim and adjust the trajectory of your shots.
Click and hold the mouse button to charge your slingshot's power, then release to fire.
Upgrade your equipment and abilities between levels to improve your chances of success.

Tips and tricks:

  • Take note of the angle and strength of your shots to accurately hit targets.
  • Utilize environmental elements such as water currents and obstacles to your advantage.
  • Collect coins and treasures to purchase upgrades and unlock new abilities.

About the creator:

Raft Wars 1 is developed by Martijn Kunst, known for creating engaging and addictive browser games.
